Right to Terminate Registration.
The Company shall have the right to terminate or withdraw any registration initiated by it under this Section 3 prior to the effectiveness of such registration, whether or not any Holder has elected to include securities in such registration.
Each such notice may result in a suspension for up to thirty (30) days.
